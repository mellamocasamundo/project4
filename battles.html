VICTORIES FOR AMERICA

Lexington and Concord
This battle led to the rebels retreating from Lexington to Concord, where they won a decisive victory.

Siege of Boston
Washington orders his troops to bombard the city of Boston, leaving heavy casualties for the British army.

Bunker Hill
Patriots set up fortifications to prepare for a British attack. When it occured,the losses on the the British side were heavy, and the Continental Army chose to retreat rather than advance.
